    \caption{\textbf{Reviewer panel composition analysis.}
    For each panel configuration, we report the mean per-paper count of:
    (i) total items the panel produced,
    (ii) unique items (no similar counterpart from any other reviewer in the panel, ordinal $<2$) -- a \emph{diversity} measure,
    (iii) items that are \emph{not} fully positive (the burden a reader or editor must triage) -- a \emph{quality} measure (lower is better),

    ``1 Human + 2 AI'' averages over all $3 \times \binom{3}{2} = 9$ panels.
