Review Settings
Enable references published after the paper
When enabled, the reviewer uses the latest knowledge including works published after the manuscript. When disabled, only references published before the manuscript date are used (date is auto-detected from the PDF, or you can set it manually below).
Leave blank to auto-detect from the PDF
Criticize items in the Limitations section
When enabled, the reviewer may criticize items mentioned in the Limitations section if they are not justifiable. When disabled, the reviewer will skip issues already acknowledged by the authors.
Maximum number of review items
Control how many criticism points (1-10) the reviewer generates. Fewer items means only the most significant issues are reported.
